<Header>

This paragraph and the two lines before this text, are included inside a <header> tag.

Header tags are used to represent information that should be on the top of your site. Normally you would include the name of your site, a little description and in most cases the following navigation:

Navigation block
  • Lists
  • Forms
  • Tables
  • Articles

<Aside>

The content included on the <aside> tag, should be information that could be removed from the page at any time and will not affect the behaviour and comprehension of the rest of the content.

For example, a list of trending topics from another website, a bunch of advertising images or links, etc.
